General Admission Requirements

Undergraduate Programs

  • Ethiopian School Leaving Certificate (Grade 12 completion)
  • Minimum average grade of 2.75 (or equivalent)
  • Pass entrance examination
  • Medical fitness certificate
  • Good moral character reference
  • Age: 18-25 years

Postgraduate Programs

  • Bachelor's degree from recognized institution
  • Minimum CGPA of 3.0 (or equivalent)
  • Relevant work experience (for some programs)
  • Two academic reference letters
  • Statement of purpose
  • Pass entrance examination/interview

TVET/Diploma Programs

  • Grade 10 or Grade 12 completion (program dependent)
  • Minimum average grade of 2.0
  • Physical fitness assessment
  • Medical clearance
  • Character reference
  • Age: 18-30 years
