About Ethiopian Police University

Excellence, Integrity, and Service to the Nation

Our History

The Ethiopian Police University was established to address the critical need for professional law enforcement education in Ethiopia. Since its inception, EPU has been at the forefront of training the next generation of police officers, investigators, and criminal justice professionals.

Our institution has grown from a modest training center to a comprehensive university offering undergraduate, postgraduate, and specialized training programs. We have consistently adapted our curriculum to meet the evolving challenges of modern policing while maintaining our commitment to ethical standards and human rights.

Our Mission

To provide world-class education, training, and research in law enforcement and criminal justice, producing highly skilled professionals who serve with integrity, professionalism, and respect for human rights.

Our Vision

To be a leading center of excellence in law enforcement education in Africa, recognized internationally for our innovative programs, cutting-edge research, and commitment to developing ethical and competent police professionals.

Our Core Values

01

Professionalism

We maintain the highest standards of excellence in education, training, and service delivery. Our faculty and students are committed to continuous improvement and professional development.

02

Integrity

We uphold honesty, transparency, and strong moral principles in all our activities. Ethical conduct is the foundation of our institution and the cornerstone of effective policing.

03

Respect for Diversity

We embrace and celebrate the rich diversity of Ethiopia. Our inclusive environment welcomes students and faculty from all backgrounds, fostering mutual understanding and respect.

04

Respect for Human Rights

We are committed to protecting and promoting fundamental human rights and freedoms. Our curriculum emphasizes the importance of human dignity and the rule of law in all policing activities.
